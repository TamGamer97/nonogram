Rules

  • Nonogram is a puzzle game played on a grid
  • Each row and column of the grid has a sequence of numbers representing the lengths of consecutive filled cells
  • Fill in the grid based on these numbers
  • Numbers indicate groups of consecutive filled cells, separated by at least one empty cell.
  • Use logic to deduce which cells should be filled and which should be empty
  • Mark cells you are sure are filled
  • In the end, you'll get a score based on how much you got right and how quickly you finished
